## 7) Odd-Service / Minutia Attacks

### ICS Protocol Scanning (Kamstrup)
- **item_id**: ODD-01
- **service_fingerprint**: Kamstrup Protocol (ICS/SCADA) on TCP/1025 and TCP/50100.
- **why it’s unusual/interesting**: Kamstrup is a protocol used in smart metering solutions (e.g., water, heat, electricity). Probing for this service is highly unusual and indicates actor interest in Operational Technology (OT) or utility infrastructure.
- **evidence summary**: 45 events were logged from 5 distinct source IPs. However, deep analysis revealed the activity was limited to superficial TCP connect-and-disconnects. No actual protocol commands were exchanged, suggesting this is automated service discovery rather than a targeted attack.
- **confidence**: Medium (for reconnaissance), Low (for targeted threat).
- **recommended monitoring pivots**: The source IPs (`3.129.187.38`, `3.143.162.210`, etc.) should be monitored for any further engagement with ICS-related protocols.
